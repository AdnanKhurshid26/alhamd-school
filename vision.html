Vision and Aim

Our vision is to impart quality education to girls and boys with a wider vision of developing their intellectual, moral and spiritual potentialities to enable them to grow into integrated persons with self esteem and positive values in life, with awareness of social justice and world peace.

Our Aims

  • 1Quality education to all, especially to marginalized.
  • 2Education to liberate from social conditioning.
  • 3Make them aware of human rights of each and every person and group.
  • 4Make them aware of the meaning of life.
  • 5Make them understand the implications of economic, political, structural and decision values.
  • 6Teach them to contribute in nation building.
  • 7Teach them to collaborate for the growth of all.
  • 8Nurtures an encounter with God as personal event.
  • 9We will learn them to empower, meet challenges and pursue goals, to encourage students to develop a sense of social responsibility.
  • 10Salient features of the school are to provide modern, scientific and progressive pupil-oriented education
